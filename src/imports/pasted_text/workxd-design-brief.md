# Figma Make Prompt — WorkXD.com

Design a modern, premium, conversion-focused website for **WorkXD.com**, a platform that helps MBA students gain real work experience through live projects with in-house brands. Students complete practical business projects, receive a co-branded certificate, gain resume-ready CV pointers, and build placement-ready proof of skills.

The website should feel like a blend of **Coursera, LinkedIn Learning, and a premium MBA career platform**.

---

# Brand Overview

### Brand Name

WorkXD.com

### Tagline

Real MBA Projects. Real Brands. Real Proof.

### Core Value Proposition

MBA students often graduate with theoretical knowledge but limited practical experience.

WorkXD helps students:

* Work on real business projects
* Gain practical experience
* Build portfolio-worthy deliverables
* Earn co-branded certificates
* Add recruiter-friendly CV points
* Develop interview-ready stories

Create a clean and professional design inspired by Coursera.

The website should feel:

* Premium
* Academic

* Modern
* Conversion-focused

Avoid:

* Dark themes
* Glassmorphism
* Crypto/startup aesthetics
* Excessive animations
